## What's a port?

A port is an adaptation of Catppuccin's palette for an app to use. Think of it as a colorscheme for a program that styles every UI component it consists of.

## What's a userstyle?

A userstyle is, in the context of Catppuccin, a port for a website which is themed via [Stylus](https://github.com/openstyles/stylus). You can view all of Catppuccin's userstyles at [catppuccin/userstyles](https://github.com/catppuccin/userstyles).

<Note>
  **All userstyles are ports, but not all ports are userstyles.**
</Note>

## Where to start

All ports should conform to our [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/catppuccin/.github/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). The staff team reserves the right to choose what ports will be included under the organization.
