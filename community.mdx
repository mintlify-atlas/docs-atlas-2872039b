Catppuccin is a community-driven project. The palette, its ports, and the tools surrounding it are built and maintained by contributors from around the world. Joining the community is the best way to stay up to date, get help, and collaborate on new ideas.

## Organization structure

Catppuccin is maintained by a staff team and a broader group of contributors. The organization structure, team roles, and governance information are documented in the [catppuccin/community](https://github.com/catppuccin/community) repository on GitHub.

## Community expectations

<Note>
  Catppuccin aims to be a neutral, welcoming environment for everyone. All community members are expected to follow the [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/catppuccin/.github/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Discussions involving religious or political content are not appropriate in community spaces.
</Note>

## Port showcase

If you have a project that uses the Catppuccin palette, you can add it to the showcase section of the [main repository](https://github.com/catppuccin/catppuccin). The showcase is a collection of community-built projects and setups that demonstrate the palette in action. Follow the contributing guidelines in the repository to submit your work.
