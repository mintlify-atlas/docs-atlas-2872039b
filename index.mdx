Catppuccin is a community-driven pastel color palette designed to be the middle ground between low and high-contrast themes. It comes in 4 beautiful flavors — **Latte**, **Frappé**, **Macchiato**, and **Mocha** — each with 26 carefully crafted colors perfect for coding, designing, and much more.
